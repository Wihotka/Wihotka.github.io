About me

Heya! My name is Paul Finagin and I'm a musician. I've been doing this for more than a decade and finally decided to make a website with my discography (I sincerely hope this will be interesting to someone). I make music, write songs, record and mix everything by myself (with a little moral help of my friends). I'm realy crazy about rock music, I listen all kindes and genres of this type of music from 50s to nowdays (and also create different themed playlists on different streaming services). But my music brings me absolutely no income, despite the fact that I put an enormous amount of time and effort (and a little money) in it.
